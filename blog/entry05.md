### Content and learning
Fore gthe past few weeks, I have been learning the A-FRAME tool. I learned how to move, rotate, recolor, and resize shapes in interesting ways. Eventually, I was able to make some simple structures out of shapes. For my freedom project, I plan to make my invention the SmartBell using A-Frame shapes.

### Skills

A skill that I gained from learning aframe was **video lesson comprehension.** Learning the tool was confusing at times, so sometimes I had to rewatch the video or really focus on it. All of this made me more able to understand video lessons where there is no teacher to help me.

Another skill that I learned is **experimentation.** My first few tries on making structures were way off, so i tweaked variables until I got it right. This helped me learn experientation as it built my trial and error capability.

### Conclusion
In conclusion, Learning Aframe has taught me how to comprehend video lessons, experiment with things, and overall be a better at learning on my own.
